About Our Product

Our project is a smart water bottle designed to help users stay hydrated by tracking water intake and providing real-time reminders. It uses an integrated sensor system to monitor the water level and syncs with a mobile app for user-friendly tracking and goal setting.

The Problem We Aim to Solve

Many individuals struggle to stay consistently hydrated, particularly those with busy schedules who often forget to drink water throughout the day. This can lead to dehydration, which negatively affects concentration, energy levels, and overall health. Our smart water bottle addresses this issue by making hydration easier to track and manage.

Supporting the UN Sustainable Development Goals

This project supports Goal 3: Good Health and Well-Being from the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals, which focuses on ensuring healthy lives and promoting well-being for all. We chose this goal because it highlights the importance of both physical and mental health on a global scale. Proper hydration is essential for maintaining energy, focus, and organ function. By encouraging users to drink water more consistently and build healthier habits, the smart water bottle contributes meaningfully to this objective.

Our Solution to the Hydration Challenge

A major challenge we identified is the difficulty many people face in maintaining consistent hydration, especially those with demanding daily routines. Our solution is a smart water bottle that uses sensor technology and mobile app integration to monitor water intake, send gentle reminders, and support users in developing long-term healthy hydration habits.

Use Cases and Target Users

The smart water bottle is designed for a wide audience, including students, professionals, athletes, and anyone looking to improve their hydration habits. It integrates seamlessly into daily routines, enabling users to monitor their water intake effortlessly. By providing a simple yet effective tool for supporting healthier living, the bottle makes staying hydrated more manageable and accessible.

From Concept to Creation: Our Development Journey

The development process began with identifying user needs to ensure the product would effectively address real-world hydration challenges. From there, we created prototypes using CAD software and carefully selected components such as water level sensors and Bluetooth modules to enable accurate tracking and connectivity. The housing was produced using 3D printing for a custom, functional design, while the system was programmed using Arduino to ensure reliability and ease of use. This thoughtful approach resulted in a smart water bottle that is both practical and user-friendly.
